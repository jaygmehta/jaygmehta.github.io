The number of conjugacy classes in the permutation group $S_6$ is 

(a) $12$<br>
(b) $11$<br>
(c) $10$<br>
(d) $6$<br>

**Solution**

We know that the number of conjugacy classes in the permutation group $S_n$ is $p(n)$, i.e. the number of partitions of $n$. Here, $p(6)=11$. Hence, the number of conjugacy classes in $S_6$ is $11$.
