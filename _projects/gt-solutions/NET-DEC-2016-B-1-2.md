What is the number of non-singular $3\times 3$ matrices over $\mathbb F_2$?

(a) $168$<br>
(b) $384$<br>
(c) $2^3$<br>
(d) $3^2$<br>

**Solution**

We know that $o(GL_n(\mathbb F_p))=(p^n-1)(p^n-p)(p^n-p^2) \cdots (p^n-p^{n-1})$. Therefore, the number of $3\times 3$ invertible (non-singular) matrices over the field $\mathbb F_2$ is 
$$o(GL_3(\mathbb F_2)) = (2^3-1)(2^3-2)(2^3-2^2) = (8-1)(8-2)(8-4) = 7 \times 6 \times 4 = 168.$$
