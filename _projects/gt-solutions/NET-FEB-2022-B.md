How many generators does a cyclic group of order $36$ have?

(a) $6$<br>
(b) $12$<br>
(c) $18$<br>
(d) $24$<br>

**Solution**

$\phi(36) = \phi(4) \phi(9) = 2\times 6 = 12$.
