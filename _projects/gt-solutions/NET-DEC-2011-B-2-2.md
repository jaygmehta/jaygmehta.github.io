Let $p$ be a prime number. The order of a $p$-Sylow subgroup of the group $GL_{50}(\mathbb F_p)$ of invertible $50 \times 50$ matrices with entries from the finite field $\mathbb F_p$, equals: 

(a) $p^{50}$<br>
(b) $p^{125}$<br>
(c) $p^{1250}$<br>
(d) $p^{1225}$<br>

**Solution**

We know that the order of a $p$-Sylow subgroup of the group $GL_n(\mathbb F_p)$ is $p\cdot p^2 \cdots p^{n-1} = p^{\frac{n(n-1)}{2}}$. Therefore, here order of a $p$-Sylow subgroup of $GL_{50}(\mathbb F_p)$ is $p^{\frac{50 \times 49}{2}} = p^{1225}$.
